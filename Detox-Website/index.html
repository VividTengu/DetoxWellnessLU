About Us

Founded in 2019 as DETOX HEALTHNESS SPA, Detox started as a drip spa, offering IV nutrition services. Soon After, additional services followed, such as nail care, massage, skin care and hair removal services. In 2021, Detox moved to its current location due to demand for more space. With the addition of its newest venture into fitness in early 2024, the Fitness Studio, Detox was voncerted into a corporation and is now known as DETOX WELLNESS.

Vision

Detox Wellness will be the premier one-stop destination for health and wellness essentials in La Union.

Mission

Detox offers the highest quality wellness and fitness services and creates innovative lifestyle programs that are founded on a holistic approach for individuals who wish to enhance their well-being. These programs include massage, skin care, healthy nutrition, and fitness.

Objectives

To establish a community destination in La Union by 2026, achieve a 90% clientele return rate by the end of the first year, and generate a substantial sales revenue by the end of the second year. Additionally, to generate a profit before tax by the end of the second year.
